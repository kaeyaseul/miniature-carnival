The Garfield Movie

Directed by: Mark Dindal

Cast of Characters:
Chris Pratt (Garfield), Samuel L. Jackson (Vic), Hannah Waddingham (Jinx), Ving Rhames (Otto), Nicholas Hoult (Jon), Cecily Strong (Marge Malone), Harvey Guillén (Odie), Brett Goldstein (Roland), Bowen Yang (Nolan), Snoop Dogg (Maurice)

Synopsis:
Indoor cat. Outdoor adventure. Garfield, the world-famous, Monday-hating, lasagna-loving indoor cat, is about to have a wild outdoor adventure! After an unexpected reunion with his long-lost father – scruffy street cat Vic – Garfield and his canine friend Odie are forced from their perfectly pampered life into joining Vic in a hilarious, high-stakes heist.
